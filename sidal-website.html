Who We Are

BUILT ON
ENGINEERING
EXCELLENCE

Founded in 2006 in Kirkuk, Sidal Company has grown to become Iraq's most trusted name in industrial water purification and air compression solutions. Our journey began with a team forged in Turkish and European industrial laboratories.

Since establishment, we've been the backbone of Iraq's industrial sector — supplying factories, pharmaceutical plants, food manufacturers, automotive companies, and construction firms with the reliable equipment they depend on.
